We're pleased to announce that [Keep the Count] is now available in the iPhone App Store.

_Keep the Count_ is for those weekend games when the electronic scoreboard for your
youth softball or baseball (or even kickball!) games isn't working, and everyone
in the stands is asking "what's the score again?", "what's the count?"

Now you can keep track with ease.  And, with the customization features, you can
show your team spirit by choosing your team's color, and selecting an app launch
icon that matches for your iPhone home screen.

See the [app's page][Keep the Count] for more information, and visit the
[App Store] to wrap your thumbs around our latest app!

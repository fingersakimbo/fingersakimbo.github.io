Fingers Akimbo cares about your privacy.  We care so much, that we don't even
collect any private data from you.  While we do analytics to better understand
what features of our applications are being used, and how, it is all entirely
anonymous, and _we never ask you for, or collect in any other way,
personally identifying information_.

[Keep the Count] and [HR Points][points] both use Microsoft [AppCenter] for analytics
and crash logging. No personally identifiable information is ever sent with
either of these reports.

[Fingersakimbo.com][fa] uses [Google Analytics] to determine website traffic. Fingers
Akimbo does not collect any identifiable information on the website, so _we cannot
and do not send any to Google in this process_.  That said, Google may have
collected information about you on other sites.  If this is a concern for you,
you should visit Google's [Privacy area] to see how to manage this data.
